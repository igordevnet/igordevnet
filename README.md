## 👨‍💻 About me

I am a developer passionate about uncovering how things work under the hood. I balance my routine between university challenges, my professional career, and the development of intense side projects. I transition comfortably between Windows and Linux (Ubuntu) environments, always looking for the best tool for the problem at hand.

My current focus is on building resilient, scalable, and high-performance systems.

### 🚀 What I do:

* **Backend Architecture & Messaging:** Solid experience with the **Java and Spring Boot** ecosystem. Currently, I build microservices and real-time communication systems using **WebSockets (STOMP)** and **RabbitMQ** for highly available distributed messaging.
* **Full-Stack Applications:** I develop dynamic and responsive interfaces with **Angular**. My latest project is a privacy-focused chat platform with support for file transfers, audio calls, and asymmetric encryption implementation.
* **Game Development & Computer Graphics:** When I'm not focused on the web, I dive into reverse engineering and modifying the Ragnarok Online engine. I work with **Shader programming (GLSL)**, dynamic shadow rendering, and wind displacement physics in foliage.
* **Infrastructure & Databases:** I orchestrate my environments using **Docker** and build persistence solutions combining relational databases (**Postgres, MySQL, MariaDB**) and NoSQL (**MongoDB, Redis**).
